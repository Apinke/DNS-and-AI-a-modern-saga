# DNS-and-AI-a-modern-saga
A comic about Domain NAme System DNS the internet phone book and its relationship with Artifical Intelligence.
Every time you go online, the Domain Name System silently translates what you typed into an address your device can use. It happens in milliseconds. It happens billions of times a day. And over 90% of malware uses it to communicate with attackers.
Most people have never heard of DNS. Most people have no idea it is a battlefield.
